About the Farmer Credit Evaluation Tool

Our Mission

The Farmer Credit Evaluation Tool is dedicated to creating equitable financial access for farmers by providing an alternative credit scoring system that recognizes the unique aspects of agricultural businesses. Traditional credit scoring often fails to account for the cyclical nature of farming income, seasonal cash flow, and the wealth of data points that can indicate a farmer's creditworthiness beyond standard financial metrics.

By incorporating GIS data, weather patterns, soil health, crop yields, and agricultural expertise, we create a more holistic and accurate assessment of a farmer's ability to manage credit. This enables financial institutions to make better-informed lending decisions and helps farmers access the capital they need to grow their operations.

How It Works

1

Data Collection

Farmers provide information about their land, crops, and practices. We integrate this with satellite data, weather forecasts, and soil analysis.

2

Credit Analysis

Our algorithm analyzes multiple data points to create a comprehensive credit score based on agricultural factors and traditional metrics.

3

Loan Matching

Farmers are matched with appropriate loan products from participating financial institutions based on their alternative credit score.
